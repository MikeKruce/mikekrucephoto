About Mike Kruce Photography

Growing up in the South Jersey / Philadelphia area, I took up photography as a hobby at the young age of 40, doing landscapes and wildlife. As the compliments grew on my photos, and I realized how much I enjoyed photography, I decided to expand into other fields. Starting by photographing some free concerts and little league games, I was able to get some attention by some local sites, to allow me to photograph some bigger venues. The more I photographed, the more I fell in love with it, and gained the attention of so many people, thanks to my work.
